The flow, end to end.

  1. 01
    Phase 01·Intake

    Lead Qualification

    A demo lead arrives. Before anything else, we verify it's a real fit for our academy — age, location, batch availability — and we tag it with a clean status so nobody loses it.

    • Verify lead quality
    • Check age & cohort fit
    • Confirm location is serviceable
    • Check batch availability
    • Assign lead status
    • Route to the right rep
    Output
    A qualified demo lead
    Status
    Owner · Sales rep
  2. 02
    Phase 02·Outreach

    Communication & Confirmation

    A fast, warm first message that turns a form submission into a booked human. WhatsApp first, in our tone, with everything a parent needs to say yes.

    • WhatsApp response within 1 hour
    • Send demo confirmation
    • Share venue, map & time
    • Share dress code & rain protocol
    • Trigger reminder system
    • Share coach & academy intro
    Output
    A confirmed demo booking
    Status
    Owner · Sales rep
  3. 03
    Phase 03·Scheduling

    Demo Slotting & Scheduling

    The right child in the right batch, with the right coach, on a day that isn't overcrowded. This is where premium experience is made or broken.

    • Assign suitable batch
    • Assign coach
    • Cap at 3–5 demos per session
    • Avoid overcrowding
    • Coordinate with ground & ops
    • Confirm slot on calendar
    Output
    Demo properly scheduled
    Status
    Owner · Ops coordinator
  4. 04
    Phase 04·Reminder

    Pre-Demo Follow-Up

    A booking isn't an attendance. The job here is to make Tuesday evening feel inevitable — a reminder, a photo of the ground, a short call.

    • Reminder message — 1 day before
    • Send ground photo & coach intro
    • Reminder call — 3–4 hours before
    • Confirm attendance
    • Build excitement
    • Capture any reschedule needs
    Output
    High attendance probability
    Status
    Owner · Sales rep
  5. 05
    Phase 05·Readiness

    Coach Preparation

    The coach knows the demo children by name before they walk in. They know what to look for, who to welcome first, and how to talk to parents at the end.

    • Coach briefed on demo students
    • Names & ages memorised
    • Session plan locked
    • One "success moment" pre-planned
    • Parent handling notes reviewed
    • Materials & bibs prepared
    Output
    Coach ready for the session
    Status
    Owner · Head coach
  6. 06
    Phase 06·Hospitality

    On-Ground Demo Experience

    The minute they step in, everything is thought-through — a person greets them, a bib is ready, a place to sit is shown. No parent should stand confused.

    • Welcome the family at gate
    • Mark attendance
    • Allocate bibs & jersey
    • Parent relations staff assigned
    • Walk-through of facilities
    • Seating & hospitality managed
    Output
    A strong first impression
    Status
    Owner · Ground staff
  7. 07
    Phase 07·Session

    Demo Session Execution

    A session that's fun, social, and lightly technical. The child leaves wanting to come back on Saturday. We observe skill quietly — we don't audition them.

    • Warm-up & ice-breaker
    • Integrate demo students with team
    • Fun technical drill
    • Game-based activity & small match
    • Observe skill level
    • Positive ending & team huddle
    Output
    A strong student experience
    Status
    Owner · Coach
  8. 08
    Phase 08·Trust

    Parent Interaction & Feedback

    After the whistle, the coach — not a salesperson — talks to the parent. Strengths, areas to grow, what the next three months look like. Honest, not salesy.

    • Coach shares strengths
    • Areas to improve
    • Recommended batch
    • 3-month growth roadmap
    • Address parent concerns
    • Hand off to ops gracefully
    Output
    Trust & conversion readiness
    Status
    Owner · Coach
  9. 09
    Phase 09·Convert

    Conversion & Follow-Up

    A same-day thank-you. A clear recommendation. Multi-day follow-ups that share testimonials and philosophy — never just price — until a decision is made.

    • Same-day thank-you message
    • Session highlights & feedback form
    • Batch recommendation
    • Fee discussion
    • Day 2–3 follow-up
    • Final decision captured
    Output
    An enrollment decision
    Status
    Owner · Sales rep
  10. 10
    Phase 10·Learn

    Tracking & Optimization

    Every demo teaches us something. Numbers tell us what worked, parent feedback tells us why. The loop closes here and starts again, sharper.

    • KPI tracking — Lead → Demo %
    • Demo → Paid %
    • No-show tracking
    • Coach-wise conversion
    • Parent satisfaction (NPS)
    • Weekly review & iteration
    Output
    A continuous improvement system
    Status
    Owner · Ops lead
